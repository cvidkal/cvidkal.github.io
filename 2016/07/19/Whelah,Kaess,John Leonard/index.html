One survey of RGB-D SLAM(Reconsturction)

发表于 2016-07-19   |  

Kinect Fusion

  • 特点

    • Volumetric voxel grid with TSDF representation
    • coarse to fine ICP alignment
  • 优点
    • 能够实时的得到良好的 mesh（Voxel grid with TSDF）
    • 动态性能好（Dense ICP alignment）
    • 重建效果好（ICP）
  • 缺陷
    • 内存静态分配，重建范围受限
    • 使用 Dense ICP alignment，运算量大, trajectory error 比较大
  • Paper

    Newcombe, Richard A., et al. “KinectFusion: Real-time dense surface mapping and tracking.” Mixed and augmented reality (ISMAR), 2011 10th IEEE international symposium on. IEEE, 2011.

    Izadi, Shahram, et al. “KinectFusion: real-time 3D reconstruction and interaction using a moving depth camera.” Proceedings of the 24th annual ACM symposium on User interface software and technology. ACM, 2011.

Whelah,Kaess,John Leonard

Kintinuous

  • 特点
    • 改进kinect fusion 的尺度问题
    • 改进 kinect fusion 的 tracking速度问题
  • 改进方法
    • 通过虚拟的移动相机使得 Voxel Grid 一直处于视野的中央
    • 将视野外的 voxel 使用三角面片来表示
    • 使用 ICP+VO 的方法来做 tracking，可以在保持 global model 稳定的情况下做到跟踪的速度和稳定性
  • 缺陷
  • 由于只在视野内使用 TSDF，视野外转换成三角面片，所以对于只对锯齿形的轨迹有较好的结果，对于小回路的轨迹model 就会不好
  • Paper

    Whelan, T., Kaess, M., Fallon, M., Johannsson, H., Leonard, J., & McDonald, J. (2012). Kintinuous: Spatially extended kinectfusion.

Robust tracking for real-time dense RGB-D mapping with Kintinuous

  • 特点
    • 使用 TUM ICCV11的 dense photometric 的 tracking 方法
    • 对纹理映射做了改进

  • Paper

    Whelan, T., Johannsson, H., Kaess, M., Leonard, J. J., & McDonald, J. (2012). Robust tracking for real-time dense RGB-D mapping with Kintinuous.
    F. Steinbruecker, J. Sturm, and D. Cremers, “Real-Time Visual Odometry from Dense RGB-D Images,” in Workshop on Live Dense Reconstruction with Moving Cameras at the Int. Conf. on Computer Vision (ICCV), November 2011.

Deformation-based loop closure for large scale dense RGB-D SLAM

  • 特点

    • 改进 loop closure 的性能，同时为了global model consistency，space deformation 做到 as rigid as possible

  • Paper

    Whelan, T., Kaess, M., Leonard, J.J. and McDonald, J., 2013, November. Deformation-based loop closure for large scale dense RGB-D SLAM. In 2013 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (pp. 548-555). IEEE.

Efficient incremental map segmentation in dense rgb-d maps

  • 特点

    • 利用对物体的移动，做到对物体的分割，得到有一定语义的 map
    • 用来应对一些动态场景（类似于 kinect fusion）

  • Paper

    Finman, Ross, Thomas Whelan, Michael Kaess, and John J. Leonard. “Efficient incremental map segmentation in dense rgb-d maps.” In 2014 IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), pp. 5488-5494. IEEE, 2014.

Real-time large-scale dense RGB-D SLAM with volumetric fusion

  • 特点
    • 改进了 Model 的表示形式，使得所有的 mesh 都用 volumetric 的方式来表示，可以做到很好的 Model，同时做到 real-time和 large-scale
      • 同时使用了 Dense 的 geometric 和 photometric 的 tracking 来保证 tracking 的稳定性和精度
      • 使用 Space Deformation 和 visual place recognition 去做 loop closure

  • Paper

    Whelan, Thomas, Michael Kaess, Hordur Johannsson, Maurice Fallon, John J. Leonard, and John McDonald. “Real-time large-scale dense RGB-D SLAM with volumetric fusion.” The International Journal of Robotics Research 34, no. 4-5 (2015): 598-626.

TUM 组

Real-Time Visual Odometry from Dense RGB-D Images

  • 特点
    • 使用 Dense Photometric 方法去做 Tracking，与 ICP 相比，速度快，并且在 small Camera Motion 的情况下比 ICP 精度要高，在 large Camera Motion 的情况下 ICP 精度高

  • Paper

    Steinbrücker, Frank, Jürgen Sturm, and Daniel Cremers. “Real-time visual odometry from dense RGB-D images.” In Computer Vision Workshops (ICCV Workshops), 2011 IEEE International Conference on, pp. 719-722. IEEE, 2011.

Robust Odometry Estimation for RGB-D Cameras

  • 特点

    • 在前面一篇的基础上使用了 Coarse to fine的方法，并且加入Motion Model 和 pixel weight实现了一种 robust odometry.

  • Paper

    Kerl, Christian, Jürgen Sturm, and Daniel Cremers. “Robust odometry estimation for RGB-D cameras.” In Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2013 IEEE International Conference on, pp. 3748-3754. IEEE, 2013.

Direct Camera Pose Tracking and Mapping With Signed Distance Functions

  • 特点

    • 使用 TSDF 来表示map，tracking 采用 local-model 的方法，利用当前的深度图去对全局的 TSDF 表示来做 tracking。

  • Paper

    Bylow, Erik, Jürgen Sturm, Christian Kerl, Fredrik Kahl, and Daniel Cremers. “Direct camera pose tracking and mapping with signed distance functions.” In RGB-D workshop on advanced reasoning with depth cameras (RGB-D 2013). 2013.

Real-Time Camera Tracking and 3D Reconstruction Using Signed Distance Functions

  • 特点

    • 使用 TSDF 来表示 map，在加上之前的 dense photometric tracker 做RGBD重建系统

  • Paper

    Bylow, Erik, Jürgen Sturm, Christian Kerl, Fredrik Kahl, and Daniel Cremers. “Real-time camera tracking and 3d reconstruction using signed distance functions.” In Robotics: Science and Systems (RSS) Conference 2013, vol. 9. Robotics: Science and Systems, 2013.

Dense Visual SLAM for RGB-D Cameras

  • 特点
    • 同时使用 photometric 和 geometric 做 tracker，加入 pose Graph 和 loop closure 做一个RGBD slam 系统

  • Paper

    Kerl, Christian, Jürgen Sturm, and Daniel Cremers. “Dense visual SLAM for RGB-D cameras.” In 2013 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems, pp. 2100-2106. IEEE, 2013.

Large-Scale Multi-Resolution Surface Reconstruction from RGB-D Sequences

  • 特点
    • 重建 Multi-Resolution Surface 用来提高 RGB-D 的重建细节精度和纹理精度

  • Paper

    Steinbrucker, Frank, Christian Kerl, and Daniel Cremers. “Large-scale multi-resolution surface reconstruction from RGB-D sequences.” In Proceedings of the IEEE International Conference on Computer Vision, pp. 3264-3271. 2013.

Volumetric 3D Mapping in Real-Time on a CPU

  • 特点
    • 提出了一种可以在 CPU 上计算的 Volumetric 3D Mapping 的方法

  • Paper

    Steinbrücker, Frank, Jürgen Sturm, and Daniel Cremers. “Volumetric 3d mapping in real-time on a cpu.” In 2014 IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), pp. 2021-2028. IEEE, 2014.

Towards Illumination-invariant 3D Reconstruction using ToF RGB-D Cameras

  • 特点
    • 提出了一种计算反射率的方法，来去除光照对纹理的影响

  • Paper

    Kerl, Christian, Mohamed Souiai, Jürgen Sturm, and Daniel Cremers. “Towards illumination-invariant 3d reconstruction using tof rgb-d cameras.” In 2014 2nd International Conference on 3D Vision, vol. 1, pp. 39-46. IEEE, 2014.

Dense Continuous-Time Tracking and Mapping with Rolling Shutter RGB-D Cameras

  • 特点

    • 提出利用 B 样条曲线去拟合轨迹，然后计算出运动参数，从而抵消 rolling shutter 的影响

  • Paper

    Kerl, Christian, Jorg Stuckler, and Daniel Cremers. “Dense continuous-time tracking and mapping with rolling shutter RGB-D cameras.” In Proceedings of the IEEE International Conference on Computer Vision, pp. 2264-2272. 2015.

CPA-SLAM: Consistent Plane-Model Alignment for Direct RGB-D SLAM

  • 特点

    • 通过约束重建的 model 中的平面，来达到更好的重建效果

  • Paper

    Ma, Lingni, Christian Kerl, Jörg Stückler, and Daniel Cremers. “CPA-SLAM: Consistent Plane-Model Alignment for Direct RGB-D SLAM.”
