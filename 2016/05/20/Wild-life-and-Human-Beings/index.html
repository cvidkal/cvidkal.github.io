Wild Life and Human beings

Hello teachers, My topic is “Wild Life and Human beings”,and what I’m going to present is “Human beings should protect wildlife!”.

Recent years, there are many kinds of wildlife die out in the world.Some of them lost their habitat, some of them drink polluted water or eat polluted food,some of them can’t adpot to the changing climate,some of them are directly killed by human for their fur or other things.

So there are many wildlife died due to Human being.But whether is their death nothing serious for us, and we needn’t to care more about their life?And Why should we protect wildlife?

Each Individual life is valuable

It is clearly that we are pleasure to make friend with some animals or have a pet.And we are also happy if we cured a injured animal,and let it go back to his own life.Why?Because each individual life is cute,beuatiful and valuable,and deserve to protect.

Protect the population more than the Individual

But in fact, the reason why we care about their individual life isn’t due to their individual distinction, it is more about our own humanity.For not every people will protect wildlife, and many people even kill wildlifes.Because the difference between two persons is much huger than two individual wildlifes.Animals or other living things inherit their appearence,physical body from their parents,and also their living method.But human is different due to our mind. We have two person who have same apperance,but we have no two person having same mind.With our mind,we can eat whatever we like,some eat meats,some eat vegetables, and we can think and live differently with any other ones,
some believe god,but some don’t.So what we protect indeed is population rather than the individual wildlife.

Protect Earth or ourselves?

Protecting the population is one important part in protecting the complexity of the global ecosystem.So we protect wildlife for protecting the earth, isn’t it?I don’t think so.The earth isn’t fragile as we think.There are some many disater in the history which makes almost all the living things in the world dead.But there are also some many lifes in our world.But our human is fragile,if the tempature gets 10 degree centigrade higher,we will die.If the sea level gets 10 centimeter higer, we will die.So if we want to be alive in the world,we must protect wildlife,must protect the biodiversity.
